Looking for older versions?





Yoshi Engine Build (2022)

The first version of Soulles DX, created by Gray in 2022 with help from YoshiCrafter29. It features outdated versions of Milk and Sunshine Encore, a few joke songs, and scrapped songs such as Prey (Good Future Mix), and Male-Addiction.






Codename Engine Build (2023)

The second version of Soulles DX, created after Yoshi Engine stopped it's development. This version features outdated versions of Ponytown, Bitchin, Azerty, and Ice Cube. This version is very unfinished, with most stages missing their mechanics and other important parts. Early versions of DLC 1 and 2 songs are in this build
