Looking for older versions?





Yoshi Engine Build (2022)

The first and original version of Soulles DX, created by Paige in 2022 with help from YoshiCrafter29. It features outdated versions of Milk and Sunshine Encore, a few joke songs, and scrapped songs such as Prey (Good Future Mix), and Male-Addiction.






Codename Engine Build (2023/LEAKED)

The second iteration of Soulles DX, created after Yoshi Engine's development ended. This version features outdated versions of Ponytown, Bitchin, Azerty, and Ice Cube. This version was left in an extremely unfinished state, with most stages missing their mechanics and other important parts. Early versions of a lot of songs songs are present in this build
